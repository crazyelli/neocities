What is Khonjin House?

Khonjin House is a web series created by Connor Lavery, better known online as Khonjin. Khonjin House started in 2015 and ended in 2016, ended again in 2019, and finally ended in 2021 The series consists of short comedy videos that are seemingly unrelated. The Khonjin House universe is absolute nonsense and anything can happen.

My Experience

iirc I discovered the series in 2016 when it originally ended. The Khonjin House movie was in my YouTube recommended and I decided to watch it because I liked the thumbnail. Little did I know, I stumbled upon the funniest web series ever. After I found out about the series, four more seasons came out in 2017, 2018, 2019, and 2021 respectfully.

Major Reccurring Characters

Khonjin

Khonjin is the main character of Khonjin House. He is invovled countless nonsensical antics throughout the series, however he is usually the cause of those antics.

Gino

Gino is the owner of Fratelli's Pizza, which is also called Gino's Pizza. Khonjin and Gino have constant rivalry throughout the series.

Gay Spaghetti Chef

Gay Spaghetti Chef is the chef at Fratelli's. However he does not know how to make pizza, only spaghetti. Also he is gay. (duh)

Gilmore (Khonjin's Father)

Gilmore is a leader of a gang including his son, Gino, and Gay Spaghetti Chef. He also seems to have mystical powers in some episodes.
