Damn Peggy

JPEGMAFIA is a underground rapper from Baltimore and is one of my favorite artists. JPEGMAFIA used to make music under the name of Devon Hendryx but has since abandoned the name. He released music under the Devon Hendryx name from 2011 to 2013, and began to use the JPEGMAFIA name in 2015. JPEGMAFIA's production is like no one elses and I believe that is what draws me to him. JPEGMAFIA has some nicknames as well including, Peggy and Buttermilk Jesus. My favorite project from Peggy is All My Heroes Are Cornballs released in 2019. I found out about him in 2018 when Veteran came out. Peggy's music is heavily influenced by internet culture too.
